Frequently Asked Questions

Is this just fear-mongering about AI?
No. This is pragmatic preparation. Even if AI never becomes superintelligent, building systems of trust and transparency is valuable.
Why blockchain instead of traditional databases?
Traditional databases can be altered, censored, or shut down. Blockchain provides the immutability and transparency that both humans and AI need to trust the system.
What if I change my mind later?
Your original declaration remains as historical truth, but you can add new declarations showing your evolution. Consistency builds trust.
How does this actually help with AI alignment?
It reduces information asymmetry. Instead of AI having to guess human intentions, it has clear, verifiable signals of cooperation.
